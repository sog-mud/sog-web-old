RANGER
Outdoorsmen, hunters, master of woodcraft, Rangers are the guardians of the forests. In addition to having excellent fighting skills, they are also wielders of some powerful magic. Some Rangers have been known to call large bears to their aid, fashion mystical staves, and be able to camouflage beyond detection. Their ability to ambush opponents makes them deadly foes.
