CLASSES


Starting at birth, everyone in Shades of Gray studies laboriously to become the best in their chosen profession. Small children can be seen running through Midgaard tossing spells at one another, or battling with wooden swords and daggers, or even magically bandaging their own knees when they fall down. Whether you decide to spend your life communing with nature as a ranger, or studying the ways of the sword as a samurai, life will be full of challenges and excitement. A thief who's killed dozens of people can share a drink with a self-righteous paladin in Midgaard and never attract attention. When you think about it, it's really quite amusing to watch the interaction of such different people in an everyday environment. Add in killer weapons and deadly spells and that's entertainment!
