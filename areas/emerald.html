All the lingering beauty of these green woods conspire to charm you into staying here forever. Woodlarks are singing high in the boughs of trees while shafts of sunlight turn the forest floor into a carpet of gold and green. The woodland trails running through this area of the forest seem well-kept, yet untravelled.
