STORM GIANT
Storm giants are the largest of the giant races, ranging from 12-20 feet in height. They are strong, and almost as durable as the dwarves. They aren't too bright, however, and their huge size makes them more clumsy than the other races. Giants make the best warriors of any race, but are ill-suited for any other profession.

Storm giants resist lightning, can fly. However, they are the most clever of giants. Storm giants, due to their size and stamina, receive the fast healing and bash skills for free. Cloud giants have weapon resistance.


The base exp for Storm Giants:
Cleric = 1600     Warrior = 1600     Ranger = 1800
