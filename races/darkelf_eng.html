DARK ELF
Dark-elves are a race of renegade elves who have turned their energy toward evil. They are inherently intelligent but have neglected intellectual studies and become very nimble from years of generations of thievery and mischief. Like elves, dark-elves have infravision and can walk unnoticed. Dark elves must stay evil to cast any spells they might know. Those who turn to good are frowned upon. Dark-Elves are burned by the touch of mithril.
