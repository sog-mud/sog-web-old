HUMAN
Humans are the most common race in the world, and make up the majority of adventurers. Although they have no special talents like the other races, they are more versatile, being skilled in all classes. Humans are able to gain more benefit from magical devices.

The basic experience for Humans:
Witch = 1000 Cleric = 1000 Thief = 1000 Warrior = 1000
Warlock = 1200 Ranger = 1200 Ninja = 1300 AntiPaladin = 1300
Paladin = 1400 Samurai = 1500 Vampire = 1300 Necromanser = 1300
