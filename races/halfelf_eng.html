HALF-ELF
Half-elves are a cross-breed between a human and an elf. They are not quite as intelligent as pure elves but have a greater constitution. Like pure elves, half-elves see in the dark with infravision.

The basic exp for Half-Elves:
Witch = 1200     Cleric = 1200     Thief = 1200     Warrior = 1200
Warlock = 1400     Ranger = 1400     Ninja = 1500     AntiPaladin = 1500
Paladin = 1600     Necromanser = 1500
