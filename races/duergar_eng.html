DUERGAR
With the recent excavations unearthing paths to the Underdark, the race of dark dwarves known as Duergar has made its way into Muddy. Like their surface cousins they are strong, though their evil ways have changed them in other areas. Unlike their cousins, they are not known for working stone and metal, something which has made them less hearty over time. They have become more dexterous through centuries of thievery and deceit. All Duergar are evil.
