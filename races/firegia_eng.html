FIRE GIANT

Fire giants are the large, ranging from 9-12 feet in height. They are strong, and almost as durable as the dwarves. They aren't too bright, however, and their huge size makes them more clumsy than the other races. Giants make the best warriors of any race, but are ill-suited for any other profession.

Fire giants resist fire . However, their slow minds make them extremely vulnerable to mental attacks. Storm giants, due to their size and stamina, receive the fast healing and bash skills for free. Cloud giants have weapon resistance.


The base exp for Fire Giants:
Cleric = 1500     Warrior = 1500     Ranger = 1700     AntiPaladin = 1800
