RACES


Shades of Gray is reknown for the diversity of its inhabitants. Not only do they vary widely by class, but more importantly, they are even more differentiated by race. Humans may be the most abundant race, but they are not nearly the only one. They have to compete with elves, elegant as graceful as their drow cousins are dark and sneaky. Dwarves also walk the land in large numbers, although they seemingly are never at peace with the giants and dragons that inhabit our land along-side them. And many other races live in Anatolia, being friends each other and enemy.


Each race has strengths and weaknesses, and these are often thought of as weaknesses and strengths, depending on who you talk to. Elves are reknown as mages and thieves, while giants are feared as mighty warriors. This in no way means that they are limited to these trades, but when choosing a race, it is important to take into consideration the stats of your favorite race and the class that you wish to be.

The culturally diverse Atatolia is home to 18 different races. Following is a brief description of each main race to assist you in the character creation process:

Human: Humans are the typical, average race, possessing no advantages nor disadvantages.
Elves: Elves are slender and fragile, yet agile and dextrous creatures. There are currently THREE elf races: Elf, Dark-Elf, Half-Elf.
Dwarf: Dwarves are typically high in constitution and strength, and have infravision.There are currently THREE: Dwarf, Duergar, Svirfnebli.
Gnome: Gnomes are typically highly intelligent but low in strength. And gnomes costs 300 extra exps per level.
Arial: Arials are a highly dextrous and smart race.They always fly. And arials costs 250 exps per level.
Giant: Giants are great and strong race. They are higher in constitution. There are currently FOUR: Fire Giant, Storm Giant, Frost Giant, Cloud Giant.
Felar: They are not strong, but has the ability to see in the dark, called infravision. And felar costs 450 extra exps per level.
Rockseer: Rockseer are fragile, agile and dextrous creatures. They can pass all kinds of doors. They are neutral, cost 500 extra exps per level.
Githyanki: Human like creatures but high in intelligent. They can be fighter, caster, or fighter/caster.Cost 250 creation points.
Satyr:
Half human,half goat race. High in dexterity but low much strong. They are good ranger and thieves. Cost 250 creation points.
Troll: Hindered by their slow intellect and mobility, grow to obtain a state of health. They cost extra 400 creation points.

P.S.: Remember that some races have their specific spells, i.e rockseers have their own spell 'meld into stone'. FOR more information try to read helps of each races.
